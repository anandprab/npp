N P Prabhakaran (എൻ പി പ്രഭാകരൻ)born on 19/11/1947 at Thiruvanchoor, a village in Kottayam District, Kerala, Indiato late Lalathu Parameswaran and Kamalakshy. He started learning Carnatic musicfrom his father since childhood. He has studied Carnatic music and Western music for 15 years. During high school periods, he began to practice Harmonium and Violinand participate in Kathaprasangam, Ballet, Ganamela as a Harmonist and as a singer. After attaining BA degree in 1970, he joined the professional drama troup Viswabharathi theatres, Kottayam as a harmonist. This entry provided to get in acquaintance with famous music composer G Devarajan Master and Kumarakom Rajappan. G Devarajan master gave a chance to work as an assistant to him and encouraged to enter into the field of music composition. Songs for the first album were composed in 1983 produced by Yuvakalasahithi, Calicut.
